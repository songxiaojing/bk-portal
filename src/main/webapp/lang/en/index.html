Hebei M.P.E. Optics Logistics Operation Co.,Ltd

　　The company is a South Korea - invested emerging enterprise. The plant is located in Huigong Road, Qinghe Economic Development Zone of Hebei province, where geographical environment is excellent and transportation is convenient. The invested projects are new synthetic optical crystal and new electronic components base; new modern information-based international logistics park. Total investment amounts to 16.25 billion yuan. Total project site covers 99.87 hectares, the planning area 120.52 hectares. The “ new optics project” is listed in 2015 Hebei key projects, ranking the 17th “ pre-project ” in the 2015 Hebei key projects catalogue .

Optical products and Logistics park

　　Logistics is compound service, including transportation, warehouse, forwarders, information, etc, playing an important role in national economy. It promotes employment, production and consumption, contributing to industrial structure adjustment, transformation of economic development mode and enhancement of the competitiveness of the national economy. The logistics management system includes customer information, logistics information, finance, OA , HR.

Optical research and production base

　　“New optics project” is listed in 2015 Hebei key projects, ranking the 17th “pre-project” in the 2015 Hebei key projects catalogue. Optical crystal is one of three most important technology in 21st century, laying foundation for high-tech and guiding its development. The information industry, aerospace, energy, biology, etc, rely on optical crystal as material base. Global economy growth and core competitiveness improvement is also enormously impacted by it. The project is programmed to build the largest production base for optical crystal and electronic component in Asia, through learning and benefiting from international leading technology, teamwork and RD power from famed enterprises. We are dedicated to making advanced products in front of global science and technology, constantly updating technology and new products, keeping optical technology in leading position.
