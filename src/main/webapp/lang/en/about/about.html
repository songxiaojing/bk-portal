Company introduction

　　Hebei M.P.E. Optics Logistics Operation Co.,Ltd is a South Korea - invested emerging enterprise. The plant is located in Huigong Road, Qinghe Economic Development Zone of Hebei province, where geographical environment is excellent and transportation is convenient. The invested projects are new synthetic optical crystal and new electronic components base; new modern information-based international logistics park. Total investment amounts to 16.25 billion yuan. Total project site covers 99.87 hectares, the planning area 120.52 hectares. Both projects are encouraged projects included in national “Guiding Catalogue of Industrial Structure Adjustment (2011 version , 2013 version ) 2015 Amendment” and “Guiding Catalogue of Foreign Investment Industries(2015 Amendment )”. The “new optics project“ is listed in 2015 Hebei key projects, ranking the 17th “ pre-project ” in the 2015 Hebei key projects catalogue .


Future Prospects

Project Plan

For Optics

The “new optics project“ is listed in 2015 Hebei key projects, ranking the 17th “pre-project ” in the 2015 Hebei key projects catalogue . Optical crystal is one of three most important technology in 21st century, laying foundation for high-tech and guiding its development. The information industry, aerospace, energy, biology, etc, all rely on optical crystal as material base. Meanwhile, global economy growth and core competitiveness improvement is enormously impacted by optical crystal. The project is programmed to build the largest production base for optical crystal and electronic component in Asia, through learning and benefiting from international leading technology, teamwork and RD power from famed enterprises. We are dedicated to making advanced products in front of global science and technology, constantly updating technology and new products, keeping optical technology in leading position.


For Logistics

　　Logistics is compound service, including transportation, warehouse, forwarders, information, etc, playing an important role in national economy. It promotes employment, production and consumption, contributing to industrial structure adjustment, transformation of economic development mode and the enhancement of the competitiveness of the national economy. The logistics management system includes customer information, logistics information, finance, OA , HR.


Development strategies

　　We aim to build green projects and a modern information-based logistics pivot, achieving effective information management and benefiting human society. The R&D institution and experts intelligence supports our projects. Domestic resources are compatibly integrated together with foreign strong powers, to build us a multinational corporation for future development. The company slogan is to create infinite possibilities. For that, we set up R&D center, information center, administration center and supply academic talents with training opportunities. The specific work as below:

  1. To manufacture new optical crystal and electronic components, making full use of existed research.
  2. To apply nano and higher technology to optical industry, improving production capacity.
  3. To enhance R&D ability, speeding up industralization and product upgrading.
  4. To refine modern information-based international logistics, increasing operation efficiency intelligently and offering superior satisfactory services to customers .
